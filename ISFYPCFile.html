Welcome to the

I See For You Puppy Club

We are puppy raisers for The Seeing Eye

Based in Passaic County, New Jersey

What we do

As Puppy Raisers, we teach puppies to be good and confident dog citizens. To do that, we provide love, care, obedience training and social exposure to each puppy we raise.

Outings

Regular outings are very important, they give us an opportunity to work on obedience with distractions and they expose the puppies to real world situations.
